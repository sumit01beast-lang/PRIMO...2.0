Who Has Made PRIMO

PRIMO has been designed and developed by Ayush Kumar Navik, a young innovator with a strong interest in electronics, robotics, and sustainable technology.

Why PRIMO Has Been Made

Created to solve the problem of high-cost assistive technology. Many schools, elderly people, and persons with disabilities cannot afford advanced robots. PRIMO provides a low-cost, eco-friendly solution for education, healthcare awareness, and safety.
